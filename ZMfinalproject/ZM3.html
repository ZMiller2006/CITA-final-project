Red Dead Redemption II

Red Dead Redemption 2 is a 2018 action-adventure game developed by Rockstar Games that focuses on the slow decline of the Wild West in 1899, following outlaw Arthur Morgan and the Van der Linde gang. As civilization encroaches, the group struggles to survive against lawmen, rival gangs, and internal divisions following a botched heist.
