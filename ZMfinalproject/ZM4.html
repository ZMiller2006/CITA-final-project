Ready Or Not

Ready or Not is a realistic, slow-paced tactical first-person shooter where players lead a SWAT team to defuse intense hostile situations in the fictional city of Los Sueños. It focuses on methodical room-clearing, strict rules of engagement, and using non-lethal methods to arrest suspects rather than just killing them.

This one is for my fellow tactical sim gamers out there. this game was made with a small team called Void Interactive. I love the realism of the game and how the game flows. If you shoot a suspect in the right spot with the right bullet they will go down in one shot. But you can't just run and gun into a building. There an be a tripwire to a Frag that can kill you and your entire team so you need to peek the door and look down to see if there is a trap. There can also be a hostage being held so you need to quickly breach the door and throw in a flashbang and shoot the suspect in the head to save the hostage.
